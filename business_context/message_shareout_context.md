## 📐 HPOV Structure & Card Architecture

The **Hero POV (HPOV)** is the primary above-the-fold carousel on the Walmart homepage.

### Card Layout
| Card | Impression Share | Primary Use |
|------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Card 1** | Tier 1 — highest priority | Top campaign, seasonal event, or major savings driver |
| **Card 2** | Tier 1 + Tier 2 rotation | Broader campaign mix, secondary seasonal messages |
| **Card 3** | Tier 1 + Tier 2 rotation | Additional content rotation |
| **Card 4** | ~17% CTR benchmark | **Dedicated to Service messages** (Pharmacy, Get It Fast, Tax Prep, etc.) |
| **Card 5** | Highest CTR benchmark (.25%) | High-performing content; often Mother's Day, Savings |

> Cards 1, 4, 5 = ~57% of HPOV impressions  
> Cards 2, 3 = ~43% of HPOV impressions (often high ad sell-through)

### CTR Benchmarks by Card (FY27)

|------|--------------|

| HPOV 2 | .15% |
| HPOV 3 | .13% |
| HPOV 4 | .17% |
| HPOV 5 | .25% |
| **YTD Overall** | **.25–.26%** (excludes Deals events) |

## 🗂️ Message Tier System

| Tier | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Tier 1** | High SOV; broadly targeted; hero-level campaigns | Easter, Mother's Day, March Savings Event, Valentine's Day |
| **Tier 2** | Targeted by persona or geography; signal-driven | Winter Weather Prep (geo), Allergy Essentials (geo), EBT Assist |
| **Service Messages** | Dedicated Card 4/5 slots; evergreen | Pharmacy, Get It Fast, Tax Prep, OnePay CashRewards Card, Subscription |
| **Static Messages** | Locked to specific date ranges; not algo-driven | Holiday express delivery, event launches, product preorders |
| **Signal-Driven** | Geotargeted based on weather or location signals | Winter Weather Prep Snow Removal (geo), Allergy Essentials (geo), Mardi Gras (Louisiana only) |

## 🔑 Key Business Concepts from Share-Outs

### Message Eligibility & Trailing Messages
- **HP Eligible Messages**: All messages approved to run on homepage (~217–399 messages at any given time)
- **Trailing Brand + Evergreen Messages**: Messages that have been live too long without strong performance
  - Weekly audit: typically 24–41 trailing messages identified each week
  - Process: Review → Business justification exceptions by Friday → HP eligibility removed Monday
  - Goal: Reduce evergreen clutter to make room for high-performing seasonal content

### Static vs Algorithmic Messages
- **Static**: Message is locked to a specific date/time slot. Not subject to algo optimization
  - Used for: Express delivery (last 24-48 hrs), product launches, event takedowns
  - Format in decks: "Static X/DD – X/DD"
- **Algorithmic**: Message competes for impressions via personalization/optimization engine

### SIG (Scrollable Item Grid)
- Sits in the BTF (below the fold) section
- Has a **parent message** + up to 9-10 **sub-messages** (different product categories)
- Accounts for ~50% of total BTF engagement
- Examples:
  - Easter SIG → sub-messages: Decor, Candy, Basket Fillers, Egg Hunt Essentials, Toys, Stuffed Animals, Hosting, Outfits, Flowers
  - Mother's Day SIG → sub-messages: Fashion, Jewelry, Gift Sets, Home, Beauty, New Moms, Top 100, Under $15, Tech, Food & Candy
  - March Savings Event SIG → sub-messages: Fashion, Tech, Home, Food, Toys, Household Essentials, Patio, Beauty, Personal Care, Top 100, New Drop

### Card 1 Takeovers
- Special strategy where Card 1 is fully dedicated to one message/campaign
- Used for: Major savings events (March Savings Event = 95% SOV), Mother's Day final push, Express Delivery
- March Savings Event WK8: 95% SOV → most dominant campaign of the season

### Ads in HPOV
- Paid ads sold through for Cards 2 & 3
- Ad sell-through affects actual SOV projections
- Examples from decks: Aquaphor, Purina ONE, Coke, Bissell, AT&T Prepaid, Colgate, Unilever, Swash

### SOV Projection Methodology
| Projection Type | How Impressions Are Estimated | Message Inclusion |
|----------------|------------------------------|-------------------|
| Monthly Projections | Equal split across eligible messages in same card | All messages known 8–12 weeks out |
| Weekly Projections | Equal split + actual ad sell-through | All messages known 2–3 weeks out |
| Actual Calculations | Real impressions per card | All messages with actual impressions |

> Projections do NOT account for personalization or individual message performance

## 🔄 Weekly Share-Out Meeting Structure

Each biweekly share-out covers:
1. **HPOV Look Ahead** — 4-week forward calendar with Tier 1, Tier 2, and Service messages
2. **Historical Performance** — LY WoW SOV and core metrics for same weeks
3. **ATF Review** — Above-the-fold visual review of messages per card
4. **HPOV Projected SOV** — Campaign SOV and SBU SOV projections (weekly + monthly)
5. **HPOV Visuals** — Per-card visual mockups with message names, owners, CTR benchmarks
6. **Scrollable Item Grid** — BTF SIG layout review
7. **BTF Review** — Trailing message audit, evergreen removal schedule
8. **Appendix** — Hero POV message lists (M0+M1 only), SOV assumptions, SIG message plans

## 🏷️ Message Naming Convention

Format observed: `[YEAR] [Campaign Name] [Variation/Persona]`

Examples:
- `2026 Valentines Day Last Minute` (Busy Families)
- `2026 Easter Express Delivery` (Online Shopper)
- `2026 Spring Baby Event Furniture Assembly Promo` (New Parents)
- `2026 March Savings Event Spring Break Ready`
- `Rollbacks And More POV` (no year prefix = evergreen)
- `OnePay CashRewards Card (In-store shopper)` (persona in parentheses)
- Signal-driven: `2025 Winter Fashion Cold Weather Shop (Fashion)+4` (the +4 = persona score/boost)

## ⚡ Signal-Driven & Personalization Notes

- **+N notation** (e.g., `+4`, `+5`): Persona targeting score/boost level
- **Geotargeted messages**: Winter Weather Prep (snow regions), Allergy Essentials, Mardi Gras (Louisiana), March Madness Champion Gear (sports fans)
- **Persona examples**: Busy Families, Online Shopper, New Parents, In-store Shopper, Fashion, Healthy Living, EBT, Sports & Fanatics, Collectibles
- **W+ targeting**: Separate messages for W+ Never Members, Churned/Expired, EBT Assist, Trial Members
- **contentservedby = `p13n`**: Personalization engine serving (vs `cxtools` = editorial)
