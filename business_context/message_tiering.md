## 🗂️ What Is Message Tiering?

**Message tiering** is the process of prioritizing messages based on their strategic value to the customer and the business. It ensures the most impactful messages receive the most prominent homepage placement.

| Tier | Definition | Placement |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| **Tier 1** | Highest priority. Nationally relevant, high volume, differentiated brand/customer experience | **Static ATF** Homepage Placement at peak |
| **Tier 2** | High priority with broad customer relevance | **Rotation ATF** Homepage Placement |
| **Tier 3** | Regionally or contextually relevant | Below-the-fold or geo-targeted |
| **Tier 4** | Not eligible for Homepage Hero POV coverage | N/A |

---

## 🔍 The 4 Tiering Factors

Every tiering decision is informed by these 4 factors:

| Factor | Question to Answer |
|--------|-------------------|
| **1. Historical Precedence / Performance** | Does this message have strong LY performance? Has it run before successfully? |
| **2. Assortment Quality / Breadth** | Is there wide, relevant assortment depth across multiple categories or price points? |
| **3. Customer Relevance** | Is this relevant to a broad base of Walmart customers, nationally or regionally? |
| **4. Omni Continuity** | Is it activated across in-store, digital, marketing, and omni channels? |

## 🌳 Decision Tree: Campaign / Category Messages

```
START HERE
     │
     ▼
Is there historical precedence?
     │
     ├── YES → Comp exposure (may test increased exposure)
     │
     └── NO  → Reduce exposure (Downgrade tier)
               │
               ▼
     Does the message have regional relevance?
               │
               ├── YES → Tier 3 (geo)
               │
               └── NO
                    │
                    ▼
          Nationally recognized holiday, specific date, OR macro trend?
                    │
                    ├── YES → Tier 1 (Static at peak)
                    │
                    └── NO
                         │
                         ▼
               Is this being activated in >1000 stores?
                         │
                         ├── YES → Tier 2 (Rotation)
                         │
                         └── NO
                              │
                              ▼
                    Is there a differentiated brand experience?
                              │
                              ├── YES → Tier 2 or Tier 3 (At peak)
                              │
                              └── NO  → Not eligible for HP Coverage
```

> 📝 *Special note:* Pet, Baby, Beauty messages use L2+ assortment span for tiering consideration.

```

     │
     ▼

     │
     ├── YES
     │    │
     │    ▼

     │    │

     │    └── NO
     │         │
     │         ▼
     │        Is it a celeb partnership or WMT exclusive?
     │         │
     │         ├── YES → Tier 1 (Paid Static)
     │         │
     │         └── NO
     │              │
     │              ▼
     │             Is the brand a market share leader?
     │              │
     │              ├── YES → Tier 2 (Rotation at launch)
     │              │
     │              └── NO  → DISCUSS (case by case)
     │
     └── NO
          │
          ▼
         Is this being activated in >1000 stores?
          │
          ├── YES → Tier 3 (At launch)
          │
          └── NO
               │
               ▼
              Is it a celeb partnership or WMT exclusive SKU?
               │
               ├── YES → Tier 2 (Rotation at launch)
               │
               └── NO → Not eligible for HP Coverage

```

> 💡 *Alternative:* Brand can be consolidated into a broader message and featured as a key item — leverage the Campaign/Category framework accordingly.

## 🔄 Tiering Process

- **Biweekly Leadership Vetting Meeting (LVM)**: Where HP Algorithm Override requests are reviewed and presented for approval
- **EVP/SVP approval**: Required for calendar lock
- **Monthly Message Calendar**: 12 weeks out; exec share-out at 9 weeks
- **Sign-off flow**: SM Leads → VP Merchant → GD Site Merch → VP Merch → EVP/SVP
